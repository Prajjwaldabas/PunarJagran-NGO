About our humanity

Transforming Lives Through Education

We teach all those children from every corner of the country, whose parents are unable to teach them, children who live in poor settlements, roam in the streets, Begging on the streets. We collect all these children and make them understand the importance of education in life and show them a way to live life and see life from a different perspective. In Bal Sanskar Kendras, along with teaching the children, we also teach them to keep the cleanliness and environment clean and to organize everything. The children of Bal Sanskar know and follow their tradition very well. The purpose of Punarjagaran Samiti is to educate all the needy children and to inculcate self-confidence in their mind and to make them aware. So that they can live their life easily and live a good life.

Years of Experience

The society was formally registered in 2014 but we have been working since 2012. It has undertaken the welfare and constructive activities in the all over the India. The society is also registered under section 80G in 2016.
